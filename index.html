The Maintenance City

What if the city was
designed to be read?

Walk past a data center and you will not know what it is. A low, windowless box. No signage explaining what it consumes, who maintains it, or whose data flows through it. This project intervenes in that invisibility.

01 Surface - The city as residents experience it. Smooth, efficient, optimized. The seduction.
02 Extraction - What runs underneath. Surveillance, data harvesting, ghost labor. The critique.
03 Proposal - The same city redesigned for legibility, accountability, and resident governance.
